About

A quiet habit of noticing.

I take photographs as a way to stay with ordinary light a little longer. The work begins in small pauses: a wall after rain, a face half turned away, a street that feels familiar before I know why.

This space is less about explaining each image than letting them gather into a rhythm. Some frames are direct, some are uncertain, and the quiet between them matters just as much.
