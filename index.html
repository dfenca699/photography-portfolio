About

A quiet habit of noticing.

I photograph fleeting moments I encounter while walking through the city — light, shadow, movement, silence, and the quiet presence of people.

Rooted in street photography, my work follows ordinary scenes that often pass unnoticed. I am drawn to moments where the everyday becomes uncertain, fragile, or quietly intense.

I continue to photograph while asking what photography can do, hoping each image leaves a small question behind.
